Our Solution:
The Cilindir Rift

The Rift allows two or more remote users to see, hear, and interact with each other as if they were together in the same room.

Additionally, the Rift enables natural, face-to-face communication with conversational AIs, like ChatGPT.

Technology Overview

We have combined a range of cutting-edge innovations into a unified platform for next-generation communication.

We call it the Cilindir Rift.

Click on the tiles below to learn more about our technology.

Shared Virtual Spaces

Cilindir enables natural interaction by placing users together in shared virtual spaces—wearables free.

Full-Body Capture

Video calls only show you from the shoulders up. VR platforms reduce you to a cartoonish avatar. Cilindir uses an integrated network of cameras to generate a lifelike 3D rendering.

Laptop Integration

Connect your laptop or mobile device to share presentation slides or to receive AI-generated text and media directly to your device.

Minimal Footprint

Engineered with an invisible hinge, the two-panel Rift unit can be folded into a corner or placed flat against a wall, allowing for easy integration into existing spaces.

Video Conferencing Integration

Whether you're joining from a Rift or a traditional computer setup, you can seamlessly participate in the same virtual spaces together with other remote users.

About Us

Our mission is to humanize how people and AI connect.

We believe the evolution of communication technologies is driven by our human need for more immediate and interactive ways to connect. As more people live and work farther apart—and as AI becomes more present in our lives—we're confronted with a new challenge: how to stay connected and build trust across physical distances and digital boundaries.

Meet the Founders

Michael Luciuk, MSc

Co-Founder and CTO

After moving out of province, Michael needed a more natural and immersive way to stay connected with friends and family back home. Instant messaging and video calls just didn't offer the immersion and realism needed for family dinners or drinks out with friends. Michael co-founded Cilindir to overcome these limitations and bring loved ones closer together.

Michael holds a Master's in Applied Computing (MScAC) from the University of Toronto, with professional experience at Qoherent (startup), BASF, Imperial Oil, and the Canadian Institute of Space and Atmospheric Studies.

Eric Macrow, CPA, MBA

Co-Founder and CEO

Eric has over five years of experience at a Big Four accounting firm, where face-to-face interactions are critical for productivity, relationship building, and business success. With the shift to remote work, Eric has experienced firsthand the shortcomings of traditional video platforms and their impact on his organization. Eric co-founded Cilindir to bridge this gap and deliver accessible remote communication solutions that effectively replicate the nuances of in-person collaboration.

Eric is a CPA, with an MBA from the University of Detroit. He previously worked as an an Assurance Manager at Ernst & Young and Manulife.
