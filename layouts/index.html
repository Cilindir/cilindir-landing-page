Our Mission

Improving remote human-to-human communication is essential for fostering collaboration and engagement in remote work, enhancing remote learning and training, and unlocking next-generation entertainment experiences. Moreover, it is essential for preserving meaningful, human connections as society transitions towards collective virtual spaces like the Metaverse.

More natural and human-like interactions are crucial for enhancing communication and building trust with AI systems, making these technologies more accessible and effective in various contexts.

Technology

360° Immersion

Cilindir offers a fully immersive 3D environment, complete with surround-screen and spatial audio, enabling more natural and expressive interactions.

Full Body Capture

Traditional platforms use cartoonish avatars. Cilindir uses a network of cameras to capture the user’s entire body, capturing lifelike detail and full body language.

No Wearables

Cilindir offers a completely wearables-free experience, eliminating discomfort and fatigue associated with the prolonged use of headsets and hand controllers.

Cross-Platform Integration

Whether you're joining from a Cilindir or a traditional computer setup, you can seamlessly participate in the same virtual meetings/spaces together with other remote users.

Charting the Path to Hyper-Immersion

The Cilindir pods provide a controlled environment, paving the way for advanced features like climate control and holography. These are capabilities that wearables can't provide due to inherent environmental constraints.
