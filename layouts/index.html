Redefining how people engage, explore, and innovate.

Our vision is for a more connected world, where everyone is empowered to experience anything.

Meet the Founders

Andrew Doucette, BComm

Cofounder and CEO

Andrew holds a Bachelor of Commerce in Logistics and Supply Chain Management from the University of Saskatchewan. Coming from a family of entrepreneurs, he has a proven track record of successfully managing and growing startups within the food and beverage sector. Andrew leads Cilindir's strategic direction, business development, and growth initiatives, overseeing all aspects of the company's day-to-day operations.

Michael Luciuk, MSc

Cofounder and CTO

Michael holds a Master of Science in Applied Computing from the University of Toronto, as well as bachelor's degrees in engineering physics and computer science from the University of Saskatchewan. With over three years of scientific development experience across large multinational companies, research institutions, and early-stage tech startups, Michael is well-positioned to lead the full-stack development of the Cilindir platform.
