Introducing Cilindir:

The next evolution in communication technology

Cilindir designs and builds cutting-edge virtual reality (VR) pods, offering a seamless, wearables-free gateway to immersive digital experiences. Each pod is thoughtfully crafted to provide a comfortable, private environment for a single user, redefining how people interact with the virtual world.

Once inside, an integrated network of cameras continuously monitors the user, powering a realistic 3D avatar. These avatars enable lifelike interactions in shared virtual spaces. When two or more remote users connect through Cilindir, they can see, hear, and interact with each other as if they are together in the same room.

What's more—Cilindir allows users to interact face-to-face with Artificial Intelligence (AI), as if it were a real person. Thanks to full-body capture, AIs can respond naturally to user’s movements and actions.

Company Overview

Problem

In-person meetings aren't always practical, and existing remote communication technologies don't always cut it.

Solution

Immersive VR pods that enable natural and engaging interactions in shared virtual spaces.

Mission

To facilitate human integration into shared virtual spaces.

Vision

A more connected world; in which everyone is empowered to experience the extraordinary.

Technology

360° Immersive Display

Cilindir offers a fully immersive 360° display with spatial audio, enabling more natural and expressive interactions.

Full Body Capture

Traditional platforms use cartoonish avatars. Cilindir uses an integrated network of cameras to generate a lifelike 3D reconstruction of the user.

No Wearables

Cilindir offers a wearables-free experience, eliminating the discomfort and fatigue associated with the prolonged use of headsets and hand controllers.

Cross-Platform Integration

Whether you're joining from a Cilindir or a traditional computer setup, you can seamlessly participate in the same virtual spaces together with other remote users.

Bridging the Gap Between Humans and AI

Cilindir enables natural, human-like interactions with conversational Artificial Intelligence (AI).

Charting the Path to Hyper-Immersion

Cilindir pods provide a controlled environment, paving the way for next-generation features like climate control and holography.

Meet the Founders

Michael Luciuk, MSc

Co-Founder and CTO

After moving out of province for graduate school, Michael wanted a more natural and immersive way to stay connected with friends and family back home. Instant messaging and video calls just didn’t offer the immersion and realism needed for family dinners or drinks out with friends.

While researching virtual reality as a platform for corporate communications, Michael realized there was a bigger issue at play: the need to bridge the gap between the physical and digital worlds. This became the foundation for Cilindir’s mission to facilitate human integration into shared virtual spaces, and our platform for networked VR pods.

Michael works as a technical lead at Qoherent, with professional experience at BASF, Imperial Oil, and the Institute of Space and Atmospheric Studies. Michael holds a Master’s in Applied Computing (MScAC) from the University of Toronto and bachelor’s degrees in Engineering Physics and Computer Science from the University of Saskatchewan.

Eric Macrow, CPA, MBA

Co-Founder and CEO

Eric's spent over five years at a Big 4 accounting firm, where in-person interactions are critical for productivity, relationship building, and business success. With the shift to remote work, Eric has experienced firsthand the shortcomings of traditional video platforms and their impact on his organization. Eric co-founded Cilindir to bridge this gap and deliver accessible remote communication solutions that effectively replicate the nuances of in-person collaboration.

Working regularly with emerging technology startups, Eric has a keen understanding of what it takes to build and scale in the fast-evolving digital landscape. Eric's brought this expertise with him to Cilindir, where he leads strategic direction, business development, and growth initiatives, overseeing all aspects of the company's day-to-day operations.

Eric works as an Assurance Manager at EY, with previous experience at Manulife. Eric holds a Bachelor of Science in Accounting and an MBA from the University of Detroit. Since returning to Canada, Eric has also obtained his CPA designation.
